Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Ace Global offer?

Ace Global offers comprehensive study abroad services including university selection, application assistance, visa guidance, scholarship support, IELTS/PTE preparation, and pre-departure briefings. We specialize in helping students pursue education in countries like the UK, USA, Australia, Canada, and many more.

How do I start my application process with Ace Global?

Starting your journey with Ace Global is easy. Simply contact us to schedule an initial consultation. During this meeting, we'll discuss your academic background, career goals, and preferences. Based on this, we'll guide you through the entire process, from choosing the right university to submitting your application.

What are the general requirements for studying abroad?

While requirements can vary by country and institution, generally you'll need:
‍
A strong academic record
Proof of English language proficiency (usually IELTS or TOEFL scores)
A valid passport
Sufficient financial resources
Relevant standardized test scores (such as SAT, GRE, or GMAT, depending on the program)
‍
Our consultants will help you understand and meet the specific requirements for your chosen destination and program.

How can Ace Global help me with scholarships?

Ace Global has extensive knowledge of various scholarship opportunities. We help you identify scholarships you're eligible for, assist in preparing compelling applications, and guide you through the entire scholarship application process. Our goal is to maximize your chances of securing financial aid for your international education.

How long does the entire process take, from initial consultation to departure?

The timeline can vary depending on factors such as your chosen country, university, and program. Generally, we recommend starting the process at least a year before your intended start date. This allows ample time for test preparation, university selection, application submission, visa processing, and pre-departure arrangements. However, we also assist students with more urgent timelines and can help you explore options that fit your schedule.
